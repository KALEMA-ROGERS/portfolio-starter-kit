Machine learning (ML) is transforming the way businesses operate. From automating processes to uncovering insights in data, ML is a key driver of digital transformation.

## Use Cases
- **Customer Segmentation:** ML models group customers by behavior, enabling targeted marketing.
- **Fraud Detection:** Algorithms spot suspicious transactions in real time.
- **Supply Chain Optimization:** Predictive analytics improve inventory management and logistics.

## Getting Started
- Collect and clean your data.
- Choose the right ML model for your problem.
- Evaluate and iterate to improve accuracy.

## Final Thoughts
Embracing machine learning can help businesses stay competitive and deliver better value to customers.
